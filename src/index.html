About the Founder

Rhett Shaw is a lawyer in the Philippines who has his own law firm, Shaw and Partners, which he operates with 6 other lawyers. They have a wide area of practice but have a good volume of operation in corporate law and business transactions. Specifically, they have had significant experience in mergers and acquisitions. They have multinational clients with businesses in Hong Kong, Malaysia, England, Taiwan, Indonesia, China, United States, and the Philippines.

He has also written laws that have now been enacted during his time working in Congress and understands business both from the government and private perspective.

While Rhett has been engaged in the practice of law for 12 years, he has also been vice-president and has either occupied board seats or key executive positions for various businesses for 8 years. He knows how to run businesses successfully.

In his first year of being a lawyer (2010), Rhett substantially assisted in the acquisition of a food brand by a large food company in Asia for $68.8 Million US dollars. Rhett has gone on to lead several other acquisitions by other large companies. He also practices citizenship, immigration, conflict resolution, inheritance, real estate, legal business consulting, among others.

Rhett has an MBA, in addition to his JD, and has a college engineering degree. He also has certificates from Yale and Wharton. He is a driven individual who has attained success as a lawyer but is passionate about this project.

NFReal is a method for selling valuable real items using NFTs. The NFT is a companion to the real asset and will exist for as long as the real asset exists. NFReal is an online auction house using NFTs that seeks to give sellers an avenue to sell their valuable items at the best possible price while providing buyers maximum, concrete, and real-world value even while having participation in the digital world.

NFReal will mint a good-looking NFT for you that will include a document in the same NFT that has pertinent details of the painting, sculpture, collectible, sports memorabilia, or any valuable asset that you wish to sell. The NFT will contain previous owner and price history, a virtual certificate of authenticity, and other relevant details (as may be applicable to the kind of asset). We will sell your asset at auction at the maximum price less our fees, taxes, and storage/delivery/insurance charges, but at no time less than your agreed upon strike price.

Unlike traditional crypto and NFTs, we will ask all of our members to submit one valid ID and fill up a KYC form. We will then validate the identity of the member. Moreover, we will give the seller the option to not pick the highest bidder. However, he must pick from the three highest bidders. We believe that some high value items are personal to the seller, and we want to give the seller the option to scrutinize the prospective buyers. There are two caveats to this ability to choose. One, the seller will have limited information about the buyer, and two, the seller must pick from the three highest bids.

We will also manually record all information and transactions in an offline database that will be updated from time to time as ownership passes from one member to another. Any information in the blockchain shall be stored in the offline database. In the unlikely event of a hack or any untoward online event, we shall restore the NFT and any records online from our offline database. This will come in handy in the next iteration of NFReal, which will include its own currency to use within the NFReal system.

Any valuable asset can be sold. We suggest paintings, sculptures, sports memorabilia, jewelry, watches, or anything that can be sold of high value. Depending on the appetite of the seller, even real property and vehicles can be sold in the NFReal.
