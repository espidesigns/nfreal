About the Chief Web Officer

Mark Louie Espedido

Mark is the Chief Web Officer at NFReal, coordinating all teams involved with both the customer-facing website and the customer portal.

For nearly 20 years prior to joining NFReal, Mark worked as a Web Designer, UI/UX and Web Developer with companies such as GO-Gulf, American Resources Group, Vanitee App, Healy Consultants, Diners Club Singapore, and had created his own web consultation company, espidesigns.

He has a wide range of clients mainly form USA--from startups, to interior designers, to travel and tours.

Mark's main focus is on creating great user experiences through clean design and intuitive user interfaces.
