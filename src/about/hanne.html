About the Legal Director

Hanne Salazar

Hanne is the Legal Director of NFReal. She is a lawyer and is currently part of a law firm. Hanne is also a licensed real estate broker and holds a Juris Doctor from the Ateneo Law School.

In her legal practice, she has been involved in special projects such as private and public procurement, purchase transactions, commercial arbitrations, and rehabilitation proceedings. She has also assisted clients in litigation pending outside the Philippines with foreign counterpart lawyers. Hanne is taking care of NFReal's intellectual property, data privacy, data protection, contracts, payments, among others.

Hanne is the epitome of consistency and diligence. She provides NFReal with invaluable reliability, making sure that as far as our system and processes are legal and that our buyers, sellers, and users are always protected.
