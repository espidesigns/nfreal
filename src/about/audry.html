About the Marketing Director

Audry Dionisio

Audry Dionisio is the Marketing Director for Nfreal. She is in charge of overseeing the development and implementation of marketing strategies for online campaigns via social media and different digital platforms.

A graduate of BS Marketing and Advertising, Audry has a vast experience in various industries. She has had early exposure to HR as an executive search associate tasked to hire top executives for different clients, with different specifications and needs.

After working corporate for some time, Audry switched gears and followed her passion for music which landed her as the guitarist for all-female band General Luna, signed under Warner Music Philippines. This has in turn opened up to more opportunities in the music industry.

Currently, Audry is the VP for Marketing and Artist Relations for the independent label Offshore Music founded by Ely Buendia, the famed Eraserheads vocalist. She is currently managing 13 successful artists under the label, and is responsible for the companies growth and businesses for the past 3 years now. Some of those business ventures are digital distribution, vinyl production and artist management.

Audry is also the head audio engineer of a studio called Crow’s Nest which she manages and runs. Crow’s Nest is a recording studio in the south of Metro Manila that has created music for accomplished musicians and bands like Apartel, Jun Lopito, Sitti, Ely Buendia and many more. On the side, Audry runs and owns her own small business that has been operational for 3 years now.

Audry thrives on multitasking and enjoys being on her toes. This fuels her passion for innovative thinking, creativity and an understanding of the consumer.
